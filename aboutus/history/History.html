History

How it started

Sweeter with love was born in 2015 from our CEO’s love for baking and making people happy. It started as a big dream in the small kitchen of her studio apartment.

We want to be there for our clients and support them in their decisions, guiding them through the process.


How it's going

Since then, the business took off, people were added to the team and in 2017 we needed a bigger space and we found the place that we call Sweeter with love’s home.
